About This Project

Hello! My name is Anina Hitt, and I'm a senior at Brown. I do a lot of peer advising, and one thing that comes up for me all the time is when people have that mid-academic crisis, where they have decided they hate their major, but also don't have a lot of time to pick up a completely different major. The reason I'm even starting this project is because I helped two people with this issue this week. Each time took me about an hour to explore all the options with my advisee, given the courses they had already taken. I wanted to see if there was a better way to efficiently see what majors you could feasibly complete in your remaining time at Brown, given the classes you've already taken.
